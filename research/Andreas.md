### German "Stiftung Kunstfonds"

* Survey with artists about A.I. in art
* Some aspects that a majority demands:
  * financial compensation, if their art was used to train an A.I.
  * Their approval for an creation of a product of an A.I. that used their art
  * Mandatory marking of or with A.I. generated content
